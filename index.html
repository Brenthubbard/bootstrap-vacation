Mount Kilimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro is one of the world's seven highest summits. It is climbed annually by more than 30,000 people and supports a robust porter economy.

I decided to go to Mount Kilimanjaro because it's the easiest of the world's seven summits. Still, Mount Kilimanjaro is no easy climb. It requires climbers to ascend nearly 20,000 feet to reach its summit.
